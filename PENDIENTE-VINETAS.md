# Viñetas pendientes de generar (capítulos 6–12)

Estilo base para todos los prompts:
> Minimalist single-panel comic in xkcd stick-figure style. Black ink lines on pure white background. No text, no speech bubbles, no captions. Simple, expressive, slightly humorous. Clean vector-like line art.

## Cap. 6 — *Diseñados para amar*
> A stick figure standing on a hilltop looking at a vast landscape. In the sky above, a simple hand (drawn larger, coming from the upper corner like the hand of God) points gently toward a small heart shape on the figure's chest. The figure looks up with a tiny expression of wonder.

## Cap. 7 — *No es bueno estar solo*
> A single stick figure sitting alone on a bench, slightly hunched. Then the same bench with two stick figures sitting together — one has just arrived and the first one is visibly perking up, sitting straighter, small radiating joy lines around both. Show both moments side by side like a before/after.

**Guardar como:** `img/vineta-cap07.png`

## Cap. 8 — *La promesa*
> Two stick figures standing on opposite banks of a river with wild wavy water. Between them, they are building a simple bridge together, each extending planks from their side that meet in the middle. The bridge is sturdy and simple. Small confident lines around both figures.

**Guardar como:** `img/vineta-cap08.png`

## Cap. 9 — *Creer en ti*
> One stick figure standing small and uncertain with a question mark above their head. Another stick figure facing them, holding up a simple mirror that reflects the first figure back — but in the reflection, the figure stands tall and confident with a small star above their head.

**Guardar como:** `img/vineta-cap09.png`

## Cap. 10 — *Sal de la tierra*
> A stick-figure house with its door wide open. Inside, a couple. Outside, radiating dotted lines spread outward in all directions like ripples in water, reaching other tiny stick figures in the distance — neighbors, children, elderly people — all touched by the ripples.

**Guardar como:** `img/vineta-cap10.png`

## Cap. 11 — *Sexualidad como don*
> Two stick figures holding hands, enclosed together inside a simple gift box outline (with a bow on top). Their free hands are open outward, giving/receiving. Small radiating lines suggest sacredness. The gift box is drawn with delicate, respectful lines — intimate but not explicit.

**Guardar como:** `img/vineta-cap11.png`

## Cap. 12 — *La familia, comunidad de amor*
> A large dinner table seen from above (bird's eye view). Around it, stick figures of different sizes — grandparents, parents, children, a baby in a high chair. The table has simple plates. All figures lean slightly toward the center. The table itself is shaped subtly like a heart, or the arrangement of figures forms a heart shape.

**Guardar como:** `img/vineta-cap12.png`
